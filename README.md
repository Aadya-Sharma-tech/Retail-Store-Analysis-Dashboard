# Retail Store Analysis

## LINK FOR DATASET ==> https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mathchi/online-retail-ii-data-set-from-ml-repository

Dashboard Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive summary of retail sales, profits, inventory, and order trends for the year 2009-10. It aggregates data across multiple countries, products, and time periods, offering insight through geographic, tabular, and graphical representations.​

**KPI Descriptions**

  - Total Sales (8.35M): Displays the cumulative revenue generated during the year across all countries and products.

 - Total Profit (6.73M): Shows the overall profit earned, subtracting costs from the total sales.

  - Number of Countries (37): Indicates the reach of the retail business, by counting distinct countries with sales recorded.

  - Total Inventory (5M): Sums up the stock held throughout the year.

  - Total Orders (4383): Counts all individual sales orders processed in the given period.

**Chart and Table Explanations**

- **1. Map Visualization**

   - Country Distribution Map: Plots the geographic locations of sales activity, using colored dots for each country. This visual highlights the global market penetration and sales density by region.​

- **2. Trend Graphs**
    - Total Sales by Month (Bar Chart): Illustrates the monthly sales trend, revealing peaks in November (1135K), December (968K), and October (965K). It helps identify seasonal effects or promotional impacts.

    - Total Orders by Month (Line Chart): Shows the volume of orders each month, peaking in November (61K) and gradually declining towards the year end. This metric reflects customer purchasing behavior and demand cycles.

- **3. Sales and Profit Analysis**
    - Sales Analysis Table: Summarizes monthly sales and profit data. For example, November reports the highest sales (1,134.88K) and profit (943,541.98). This table enables quick comparison of financial performance month over month.

    - Total Profit by Month (Bar and Pie Charts): Both bar and pie charts visualize profit distribution across months. November is the most profitable, followed by December and October, underlining crucial months for business revenue.

- **4. Categorical Breakdowns**
    - Country-Level Table: Lists countries and their total item quantity sold. Denmark shows the highest volume (227,030), reflecting either high demand or frequent orders.

    - Monthly Sales vs Quantity Sold (Bar Chart): Compares sales revenue against item volumes for each month, unveiling efficiency and average item price fluctuations.

- **5. Donut Charts**
    - Total Sales by Month: Displays percentage contributions of each month to total sales, highlighting the most lucrative months.

    - Total Quantity by Month: Shows the share of products sold by month, making it easier to correlate sales spikes with product volumes.

 **Dataset Structure**
The dataset consists of detailed transaction records with fields including:

- Invoice number

- Stock code

- Product description

- Quantity sold

- Invoice date/time

- Unit price

- Customer ID

- Country

- Year and month
